CREATIVE WORK

In my everyday job as a creative in the agency I have an amazing opportunity to use my knowledge and experience to create unique concepts for our clients from all around the world including: Netflix, TikTok and Klarna.

Strangers Things Zone 2022

Netflix event stand for Orange Warsaw Festival promoting Stranger Things season 4. The main task for the agency was to create unique space that can essentially transport visitors into Upside World with multiple interactive elements such as: multiplayer movie screen powered with bicycles, photo opportunities, messages outdoor zone and chill zone with DJ booth.

KLARNA EXPO 2022

Project design for series of business EXPO's for Klarna. The final design focused on the idea of office space where people and creative ideas meet. One of the most profound elements was grand stairs where representatives of the company held the official meetings with clients. The whole set was complemented by contrasting modernist furniture, a display case for the competition and a counter with matching branded fridge for cold beverages.

TIK TOK MODULAR STAND 2022

Project designed for TikTok, consisting of a series of modular elements, reusable at various events promoting the brand for the 2022-2023 season.

HANIA RANI 2019

During my work as an Event Coordinator at Festival of Open Flats Cracow, I had an amazing opportunity to create unique cameral concert in Mehoffer's Garden for singer, composer and pianist Hania Rania.

Queen Premiere 2022

Venue design for Netflix premier launch of Queen mini series. Project included: creative concept, location selection, set design, selection of influencers and invited stars, space arrangement, gift proposal, key visuals for the event, invitations.

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Implementation of projects on request for clients from various industries has always been an interesting challenge that I have always undertaken. Each of the projects is a new opportunity to learn and improve the workshop, behind which there are many hours of creative concepts.

Florence + The Machine 2022

For this year Orange Warsaw Festival 2022, I had opportunity to create gig poster for Florence and The Machine. For this particular project inspired by traditional festival called Kupala Night, I went back to my origins with digital hand drawing. The signed art work will go to an international charity auction supporting humanitarian operations in Ukraine.

TOVE LO 2022

Cover design for the newest single of the singer Tove Lo "No one dies from love". The project was created by combining 3d visualization, motion design and traditional techniques of digital graphics using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

CASABLANCA

Graphic design using 3d animations and motion design, made for the Casablanca fashion company, to promote the latest collection for the 2023 season.

Bel Rebel perfume 2021

A series of 3D graphics and visualizations commissioned by the Bel Rebel company. The project can be viewed on their interactive website.
